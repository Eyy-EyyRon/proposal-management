1. The Command & Control Workflow

A four-layer delegation chain. Every CEO-tasked proposal is drafted, verified, and polished before the CEO ever sees it.

STEP 1
👑

CEO Creates Task

Sets client, urgency (P1–P3), template & assigns to General Admin.

STEP 2
🛡

Admin Routes

Assigns to Dept Admin who picks the Staff member to build the draft.

STEP 3
⚙️

Staff Builds & Dept Admin Verifies

Staff drafts, submits. Dept Admin approves or requests revision.

STEP 4
✅

CEO Talks & Sends

Verified proposal appears in Talking Inbox. CEO reviews & sends to client.

3. Urgency Tiers & SLA Timers

Every task gets an urgency level when the CEO creates it. The SLA timer counts down in real-time across all dashboards.

P1

Critical Priority

⏱ 2-hour SLA

Reserved for the most time-sensitive opportunities. Entire UI enters focus mode.

UI: Red pulse ring + animated "CRITICAL TASK" banner with live countdown at top of proposal.
Task Board: Pinned to top of all boards in rose section with animated ring around each card.
Alert: Toast fires when <30 min remaining. Cannot be missed.
P2

High Priority

⏱ 24-hour SLA

Important deals requiring same-day attention. Highlighted in orange throughout the interface.

UI: Orange border on task cards. Countdown turns amber when <6 hours remain.
Task Board: Standard order, orange-bordered card.
Alert: Live countdown visible on all related views.
P3

Standard Priority

⏱ 72-hour SLA

Regular pipeline proposals. Grey countdown badge, no special alerts.

⚠ SLA Breach

When the timer hits zero, the badge turns red and shows "OVERDUE by Xm". The task remains fully actionable — SLA breach is a tracking indicator, not a lock.

👑 CEO Guide — Root Authority

Your dashboard only shows what requires direct CEO attention. The team handles everything else.

Creating a Delegated Task

1

Task Center → + New Task

Navigate to /ceo-dashboard → Task Center.

2

Fill the Task Brief

Enter client name, email, select template, write brief, set urgency (P1/P2/P3).

3

Assign to General Admin

Pick from org's admin list. They receive an in-app notification immediately.

4

Wait for Talking Inbox

When Dept Admin verifies, the proposal auto-appears with a Ready to Send badge.

5

Review & Send

Open the proposal, review the document, click Mark as Sent. Client receives secure portal link.

🔒 Privacy Shield

Toggle Private on any proposal — invisible to all Admins and Staff. Overrides all sharing settings.

♾ Immortality Logic

The CEO account cannot be deleted, downgraded, or transferred. Protected at Firestore security rules level.

📋 Direct Proposals

Create and send proposals yourself without delegation — use the standard proposal builder for quick deals.

📊 Full Analytics

Access conversion rates, monthly volume, response times, and performance metrics across all departments.

Talking Inbox: Only ready_to_send proposals appear here. Drafting and verifying proposals are intentionally hidden.

🛡 General Admin — Routing Layer

First delegation point after the CEO. Receive tasks, choose the right department, monitor delivery.

1

Check Notifications

In-app notification arrives when CEO assigns a task. Click the bell icon.

2

Task Board → Needs Routing

P1 tasks are pinned at the top with a red pulse ring. Check the SLA countdown.

3

Click "Assign Dept Admin"

Select the Dept Admin. They inherit the urgency level and SLA timer.

4

Monitor via In Progress

The full delegation chain (Dept Admin → Staff → Status) is visible on the task card at any time.

🏢 Dept Admin — Quality Gate

Final quality checkpoint before the CEO sees anything. Nothing passes without your approval.

1

Assign Staff from Needs Routing

Click Assign Staff. Only staff in your department are shown.

2

Wait for Staff Submission

Task moves to Verification Queue — department-locked, only visible to you.

3

Review Proposal

Open the proposal. Read the document. Check the violet Internal tab for staff notes (hidden from client).

4a

Approve → Verify & Promote

Quality looks good? Click Verify & Promote. Status becomes ready_to_send and lands in CEO Talking Inbox.

4b

Request Revision

Click Request Revision, write specific feedback. Staff sees an amber banner on the proposal with your notes.

Department Silo: Verification Queue is dept-locked. You only see proposals from your own department unless cross-dept share is explicitly granted.

⚙️ Staff Guide — Precision Builder

Draft proposals in CEO voice. You submit — never send directly to the client on tasked proposals.

1

Check My Tasks

/dashboard/tasks → tasks appear in In Progress. P1 tasks need immediate action.

2

Open Linked Proposal

Click View Proposal on the task card.

3

Write in CEO Voice

A purple banner reminds you: "Write as if the CEO is speaking directly to the client." Fill all dynamic fields.

4

Use Internal Tab for Notes

The violet Internal tab lets you leave notes for your Dept Admin — completely hidden from the client.

5

Submit for Verification

Click the blue Submit for Verification button (replaces Send on tasked proposals). Routes to Dept Admin's queue.

Revision Requested

  • Amber banner shows admin's feedback at the top of the proposal
  • Task moves to Revision Requested section on your board
  • Edit the proposal → re-click Submit for Verification
  • Do not contact the client during this phase

🚫 You cannot send a tasked proposal to the client.

The Send button is intentionally hidden on CEO-tasked proposals. All client communication flows through the CEO after verification.

🔗 Cross-Department Sharing

Proposals are department-scoped by default. Explicitly share for cross-team collaboration.

👁

View Only

Read-only access. Cannot edit or submit for verification.

✏️

Can Edit

Can edit the draft. Verification is always locked to the origin department admin.

To share: open proposal → click the Share (chain icon) button → select departments & access level → Save. Only admins and the CEO can manage sharing.
